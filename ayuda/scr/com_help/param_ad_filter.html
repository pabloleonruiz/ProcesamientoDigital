Description

Improved version of anisotropic diffusion filter. Parametric anisotropic diffusion filter also iteratively removes noise from the <image>, while calculating diffusion coefficient, in other words amount of diffusion, during run-time, at each iteration. Using the blocks whose size is specified by <block_size>, variance is calculated, and from there, the most homogeneous region is selected to calculate the diffusion coefficient.
