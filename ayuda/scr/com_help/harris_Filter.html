Description

     The Harris filter is a spatial-domain method for for corner

     detector developed by Harris and Stephens in 1988.In this    

     filter corner response function to find the corners.

 

 

     Where CRF(r,c) represents the corners  of  the original  image 

     And G(P1*P1) is the Gaussian of the vertical gradient. G(P2*P2)

     Is the Gaussian of horizontal gradient.
